My Books

Fun fact about me: I write, edit, and self-publish books! I started self-publishing when I was 25 and completed my first short story collection in 2022.

The Moonlight Thrills Series is a paranormal romance series set in a world where magical species (werewolves, vampires, fairies, wizards, etc.) publicly coexist with humans. Each book can be read as a standalone, but it's best to read them in order because some characters make appearances in other stories.

Meryle and Lancelot's Adventures is a short story series that reimagines the Arthurian legends. Join Meryle Evanders, Merlin's cousin, and Lancelot as they undertake magical quests, fall in love, and discover Merlin's plan for Camelot.
