My Books

Fun fact about me: I write, edit, and self-publish books! I started self-publishing when I was 25 and completed my first short story collection in 2022.

I loved writing when I was a kid.

The Moonlight Thrills Series is a paranormal romance series with a dash of thrills (See what I did there?). Each book can be read as a standalone, but it's best to read them in order because some characters make appearances in other stories. Meryle and Lancelot's Adventures is a reimagining of the Arthurian legends. Meryle Evanders, Merlin's cousin, is the main protagonist of the story. There is a slow-burn romance between the main characters and (of course) magical adventures! All of my eBooks are free. They're available on: Apple Barnes & Noble Kobo Kobo Plus Scribd Thalia Mondadori Vivlio Overdrive (for libraries) Palace Marketplace (for libraries) Even if you're not a fan of romance, fantasy, or the paranormal genre, give my books a try!
